UFO Silver Shadow

A lamp that disappears into your space — until you need it. Brushed silver finish complements concrete, wood, and stone.

Designed for the in-between

Not quite a floor lamp. Not quite a pendant. The UFO Silver Shadow lives in the space between — literally and metaphorically. Its telescopic stem lets it hover at counter height for cooking, drop low for reading, or rise tall for ambient room light. The silver shadow finish reflects surrounding colors, making it a chameleon in any interior.
