About Dornori

Where it started

Dornori was born at a beach gathering. A group of friends — guitars out, food spread across a blanket, the sky going dark — had no good light. Phones came out. White LED glare killed the mood. Someone said: "I just want to see your face."

That one moment became the Star‑A: a portable outdoor lamp that rises on command, runs on batteries, and looks like nothing you've seen before.

What makes it different

  • ✅ It moves. The pole rises.
  • ✅ Real components. DC motor, Faston connectors, Wago lever terminals.
  • ✅ Off-the-shelf. Every electronic component is industry-standard.
  • ✅ Outdoors by design. 3× protected 18650 batteries.
  • ✅ Two models. The UFO and the Mushroom.
  • ✅ Open files. STL, OBJ, 3MF, schematics available.
  • ✅ Repairable. Every part available separately.

Who builds (and uses) a Star‑A

The Star‑A was designed for builders aged 8–12 working with an adult. In practice, it finds its way to kids, families, grandparents, classrooms, makers, and anyone who wants portable outdoor light they built themselves.

Three ways to get a Star‑A

  • Ready to use — fully assembled.
  • Complete Assembly Kit — pre-printed parts + electronics.
  • 3D Print Files — download and print yourself.
