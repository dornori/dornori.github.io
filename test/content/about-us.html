About Dornori

Dornori was born at a beach gathering. A group of friends — guitars out, food everywhere, the sky going dark — had no good light. Phones came out. White LED glare killed the mood. Someone said: "I just want to see your face."

That one moment became the Star-A: a portable outdoor lamp that you build yourself. A pole rises from a compact base, lifting a glowing sculptural head into the air above your space — powered by batteries, driven by a real DC motor, wired with your own hands. No fire, no power outlet, no disposable toy.

We designed the Star-A specifically for builders aged 7–12 working with an adult — parents, teachers, mentors, or workshop leaders. It's a real engineering project with real components: DC motors, Faston connectors, Wago lever terminals, gear assemblies, and 3D-printed structural parts. Everything works the way real things work.

There's something that happens the first time a child flips the lever and watches the pole rise, carrying a glowing UFO or mushroom into the air. Their eyes go wide. Then they say: "Wait — I built that?" That moment is what Dornori exists for.

Who Builds a Star-A?

  • 🧒 Kids aged 7–12 building with a parent, guardian, or teacher
  • 👨‍👧 Families who want a Saturday project that actually does something
  • 🏫 Classrooms and STEM programs — consistent, documentable, genuinely exciting
  • 🧩 Special needs maker spaces — tactile, step-by-step, rewarding at every stage
  • 🖨️ Maker families with 3D printers — print, source, build, take outside
  • 🌙 Anyone who wants a lamp they can take to a beach night, a park, or a camping trip — and say they built it themselves

What Makes It Different

  • ✅ It moves. The pole rises. The lamp head climbs. There's nothing passive about it.
  • ✅ Real components. Wago, Faston, DC motor, gear assembly — not toy-grade shortcuts.
  • ✅ Outdoors by design. Battery-powered (3× 18650), portable, built for real use.
  • ✅ Two models. The UFO and the Amanita mushroom — same electronics, different magic.
  • ✅ Open files. Every STL, schematic, and wiring diagram published.
  • ✅ Repairable forever. Every part sold separately. No planned obsolescence.
