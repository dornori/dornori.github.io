The lamp that rises.

The Star‑A is a kinetic, battery-powered outdoor lamp unlike anything else. A pole extends from a compact base — carrying a glowing UFO or Mushroom lamp head up to 190 cm into the air — driven by a real DC motor, controlled by a simple lever switch. No fire. No power outlet. No app. Just flip a lever and watch it rise.

Why the Star‑A?

✅
It moves — the pole rises, the lamp climbs. Nothing else does this.
✅
Truly portable — 100 × 100 × 70 mm base, runs on 3× 18650 batteries.
✅
Real electronics — DC motor, Faston connectors, Wago terminals. Industry-standard, repairable.
✅
Open source — free STL, OBJ, 3MF files for every part. Build, modify, share.
✅
Two models — UFO and Mushroom. Four colourways each.
✅
Built to last — every part individually replaceable. No planned obsolescence.
