Star‑A is a kinetic outdoor lamp that rises from your bag, extends to 190 cm, and runs all night on batteries. Build one yourself — or give one ready-made.

What is the Star‑A?

A 100 × 100 × 70 mm base. Flip the lever up — the pole extends to 190 cm, carrying a glowing UFO or Mushroom lamp head into the air above wherever you are. Flip it down — it retracts back to compact. Battery-powered, portable, kinetic. Two models, eight colourways. Made to be taken outside.

Three ways to get one

  • 🚀 Ready to use — fully assembled, load batteries, flip the lever.
  • 🛠️ Complete Assembly Kit — build in 90 minutes, no soldering, no 3D printer needed.
  • 🖨️ 3D Print Files — print every part yourself, full maker control.
