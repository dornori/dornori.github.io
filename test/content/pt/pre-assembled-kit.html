🧠 The Natural Path: Play → Curiosity → Build

Stage 1 — Wonder (first few weeks): Flip the lever. Watch it rise. Flip it back. Watch it retract. Do this approximately forty times. Ask "how does it do that?"

Stage 2 — Curiosity (weeks 2–6): Read the included "How It Works" booklet. Spot the motor. Figure out that the lever controls which way the motor spins. Start asking about circuits.

Stage 3 — Builder (when they're ready): Upgrade to the Complete Assembly Kit and build their own from scratch. Now they know what every part does before they even open the bag. A discount code for the kit is included in the box.
