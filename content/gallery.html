A look at Dornori in the wild — classrooms, kitchens, living rooms, and late-night garden sessions.

Every image here is real. Real kids, real builds, real reactions. No staging, no stock photos.

In the Classroom

Schools and makerspaces have been using the Star-A Build Kit as an introduction to electronics and mechanical assembly. Teachers tell us the moment the lamp head rises for the first time is when the room goes quiet — and then immediately loud.

At Home

Most builds happen at the kitchen table. One side has the instructions, the other has a pile of parts. Somewhere in the middle is a child who has just discovered that they can make something that actually works.

Outside

The Star-A is battery-powered and built for outdoor use. It has lit up campsites, balconies, beach nights, and at least one birthday party in a garden where the adults were more impressed than the kids.
