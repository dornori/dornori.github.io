Cookie Policy

Effective Date: March 31, 2026

This policy explains how dornori.com uses cookies and similar technologies. Under the EU ePrivacy Directive and GDPR, we require your prior, freely given consent for all non-essential cookies. You may withdraw or change your consent at any time.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. Similar technologies include web beacons, pixel tags, and local storage. They are used to make websites function, remember your preferences, and provide usage information to the operator.

2. Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are required for the Website to function and cannot be switched off. They do not require your consent.

  • session_id — Maintains your session. Duration: session.
  • cart_token — Shopping cart persistence. Duration: 30 days.
  • csrf_token — Cross-site request forgery protection. Duration: session.
  • __cf_bm — Bot management (Cloudflare). Duration: 30 minutes.

3. Functional / Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your choices to improve your experience. They require your consent.

  • user_prefs — Currency, language, and display preferences. Duration: 1 year.
  • cookie_consent — Records your consent choice. Duration: 1 year.

4. Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use the Website. Data is aggregated and anonymised where possible. They require your consent.

  • _ga — Google Analytics, distinguishes users. Duration: 2 years. Set by: Google.
  • _ga_* — Google Analytics, session state. Duration: 2 years. Set by: Google.

5. Marketing & Advertising Cookies

We currently do not place marketing or advertising cookies. This section will be updated if that changes. We do not use behavioural advertising cookies in relation to users we identify as children under any circumstances.

6. Your Consent

On your first visit a cookie consent banner will request your consent for non-essential cookies. Rejecting optional cookies is as easy as accepting them — both actions require a positive click. Your choice is stored and respected on subsequent visits.

You may withdraw or change your consent at any time by clicking "Cookie Settings" in the website footer, adjusting your browser settings (see Section 7), or contacting privacy@dornori.com.

7. Managing Cookies via Your Browser

Most browsers allow you to view, manage, delete, and block cookies. Blocking essential cookies will impair Website functionality.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

8. Children & Cookies

We do not use analytics, advertising, or profiling cookies in relation to users we identify as children (under 13 in the US; under 16 in the EU/UK). Parental consent is required before any non-essential cookies are placed in connection with a child's use of our services. See our Children's Privacy Notice.

9. Changes

We may update this policy at any time. Material changes will be notified via the Website. The effective date above reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact

For cookie-related questions: privacy@dornori.com
