Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 31, 2026

This policy applies globally to all products and services offered through dornori.com. The legal operator is identified by the ICANN-registered Administrative Registrant for the domain dornori.com. This policy is designed to comply with GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, COPPA, PIPEDA, LGPD, and Australia's Privacy Act 1988.

1. Definitions

Personal Data — any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person. Processing — any operation performed on Personal Data. Controller — the ICANN-registered operator of dornori.com. Child / Minor — any individual under 13 (US/COPPA), under 16 (EU/UK GDPR), or such higher age as required by local law.

2. Information We Collect

Provided directly: name, email address, postal/shipping/billing address, phone number (if voluntarily given), order details, and content included in communications when you create an account, subscribe to newsletters, place an order, or contact us.

Automatically collected: IP address and approximate geolocation, browser type and version, device identifiers, operating system, referring URLs, pages viewed, time spent, and interaction data.

Transaction data: order details, billing/shipping information, and payment confirmation tokens. Payment card data is processed exclusively by PCI-DSS compliant third-party processors (Stripe, PayPal). We do not store full payment credentials.

Hardware device data: where Dornori hardware connects to digital services, we may collect device usage data, firmware version, and interaction logs. Product-specific details are in the relevant product documentation.

3. Purposes & Legal Bases

We process Personal Data to operate and maintain the Website (legitimate interests); fulfil orders and provide customer support (contract performance); send newsletters you have subscribed to (consent); conduct analytics and improve performance (legitimate interests / consent); prevent fraud and ensure security (legal obligation / legitimate interests); and comply with applicable law (legal obligation).

Where we rely on legitimate interests, you have the right to object. See Section 7.

4. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect Personal Data directly from children under 13 (US) or 16 (EU/UK) without verified parental consent. We apply enhanced protections to children's data including no behavioural advertising, no sale of children's data, and parental access and deletion rights. Full details are in our Children's Privacy Notice.

5. Newsletter & Marketing

By subscribing you consent to receive recurring emails which may include product updates and promotional content. You may unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email or by contacting privacy@dornori.com. We do not send marketing emails to addresses associated with child accounts.

6. Cookies & Tracking

Essential cookies operate without consent. All other cookies require your prior, freely given consent, which you may withdraw at any time. Full details including a cookie inventory are in our Cookie Policy.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your Personal Data; object to processing; withdraw consent; and (California) opt out of sale or sharing. To exercise any right, contact privacy@dornori.com. We respond within 30 days (GDPR) or 45 days (CCPA). We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

EU/UK residents may lodge a complaint with their local supervisory authority. Find yours at edpb.europa.eu.

8. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your Personal Data. We may share data with: infrastructure providers (Cloudflare); payment processors (Stripe, PayPal); email and newsletter services; analytics providers (aggregated/anonymised where possible); logistics and fulfilment partners; and legal authorities when required by law. All processors are bound by Data Processing Agreements. A full Subprocessor List is maintained.

9. International Transfers

Data may be transferred outside your country. Transfers from the EEA, UK, or Switzerland are protected by EU Standard Contractual Clauses, UK International Data Transfer Agreements, or adequacy decisions as applicable.

10. Data Retention

Communications and support records: up to 2 years after resolution. Newsletter data: until unsubscribed plus 30 days. Order and transaction data: 7 years minimum (tax/legal obligations). Analytics data: up to 26 months. Fraud prevention records: up to 5 years.

11. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS 1.2+ encryption in transit, access controls, and regular security assessments. In the event of a personal data breach we will notify the relevant authority within 72 hours and affected individuals without undue delay where required. See our Security Policy.

12. EU/UK Right of Withdrawal

EU and UK consumers have a 14-day right of withdrawal under the Consumer Rights Directive (2011/83/EU) and UK Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013 unless an exception applies. For hardware returns see our Returns & Refunds Policy.

13. Changes

Material changes will be notified via email (to registered users) or by prominent website notice at least 30 days before taking effect. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

14. Contact

All privacy requests and inquiries: privacy@dornori.com
