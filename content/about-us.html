About BuildBox

Welcome to BuildBox, a brand dedicated to turning screen time into make time. Founded by engineers and parents, we believe every child deserves the spark of "I BUILT THIS!"

We started BuildBox in 2022 after realizing that most "educational" STEM toys are oversimplified black boxes. Kids press buttons, lights flash, and they learn nothing about how it actually works. So we created something different: real components, real circuits, real pride.

Every BuildBox is designed for 90 minutes of genuine parent-child bonding. No screens. No frustration. Just real learning, real laughter, and a finished project that your child will show off for years.

We're proudly based in Austin, Texas, with a small but mighty team of 12 makers, educators, and parents. Our parts are sourced from ethical suppliers in Japan, Germany, and the USA. Every electronics kit is hand-tested before shipping.
