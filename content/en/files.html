What's included

Every 3D-printed structural part for both the UFO and Mushroom models — optimised for FDM printing, no supports needed on most parts.

  • Base body (motor + battery housing)
  • Tube sections × 3 (telescoping pole)
  • Gear housing + motor bracket
  • UFO saucer head (lamp holder)
  • Mushroom cap (lamp holder)
  • Cable routing clips
  • Wago terminal bracket

Print settings

  • Material: PLA or PLA+ (PETG also works)
  • Infill: 20–30% — gyroid or grid
  • Layer height: 0.2mm standard / 0.15mm quality
  • Supports: None required on most parts
  • Print time: ~14 hours total (all parts)
