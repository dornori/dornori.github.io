Product Embed Flags

Use data-variants and data-related flags to control what features appear in product cards. By default (no flags), cards show only the product image, name, price, and "Add to Cart" button.

Default (Minimal)

<div data-shop-products="ufo-spaceblue"></div>

With Variants Flag

<div data-shop-products="pre-assembled" data-variants></div>

With Related Flag

<div data-shop-products="electronics-bundle" data-related></div>

All Flags Combined

<div data-shop-products="pre-assembled" data-variants data-related></div>
