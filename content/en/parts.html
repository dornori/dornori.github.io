Replacement & Spare Parts

Every part of the Star‑A is available separately.  |  Coming Soon

The Star‑A is built entirely from off-the-shelf, industry-standard components. Nothing is proprietary. If something wears out, gets upgraded, or needs replacing — you don't need to buy a whole new lamp.

Electronic components

DC motor (12 V)
3-position lever switch (Up–0–Down)
Power switch with integrated LED indicator — available in multiple colours
3 W LED Star lamp head
18650 battery charger
Protected 18650 battery cells (sold individually)
Wago connector sets
Colour-coded wire harness
Faston crimp connectors and ring terminals

Printed structural parts

All mechanical components — base housing, tube sections, gear housing, motor mount, lamp head (UFO and Mushroom) — are available individually. If you'd rather print your own, the 3D Print Files are available for download.

Hardware

  • M3 screw and fastener set
  • Gear assembly replacement set

Can't find what you need?

Every component in the Star‑A is documented in the Bill of Materials (included with the 3D Print Files download and the Complete Assembly Kit). All components are standard off-the-shelf items available at electronics retailers and hardware stores worldwide.
