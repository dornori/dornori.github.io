Consumer Rights

Returns, Refunds & Warranty

Effective Date: March 31, 2026  ·  Document version 1.0

Your statutory consumer rights under EU, UK, Australian, and other applicable laws are not limited by this policy. Where any provision herein conflicts with applicable mandatory consumer law, your statutory rights prevail. To initiate a return: support@dornori.com

1. EU & UK — 14-Day Right of Withdrawal

If you are a consumer located in the European Union or United Kingdom, you have the right to withdraw from your purchase of hardware products within 14 calendar days of the date you (or a third party you designate) physically receive the goods, without providing any reason.

How to exercise your withdrawal right:

  1. Notify us of your decision to withdraw by emailing support@dornori.com before the 14-day period expires. You may use the subject line "Withdrawal Notice — Order #[number]".
  2. Return the goods within 14 days of sending your withdrawal notice, in their original condition and packaging where possible.
  3. You bear the direct cost of returning goods unless the goods are defective or we shipped incorrectly.

We will refund all payments received, including standard delivery charges (not express or premium shipping surcharges), within 14 days of receiving the returned goods or proof of return postage, using the original payment method.

We may reduce the refund amount if the goods have been handled beyond what is necessary to establish their nature, characteristics, and functioning.

Exceptions to withdrawal right:

  • Goods made to your personal specification or clearly personalised
  • Sealed goods that are not suitable for return for hygiene or health protection reasons, where the seal has been broken after delivery
  • Goods that are inseparably mixed with other items after delivery

2. Defective Goods & Legal Warranty

2.1 EU / UK Statutory Warranty (2 Years)

Under EU Directive 2019/771 and the UK Sale of Goods Act / Consumer Rights Act 2015, all hardware products carry a minimum 2-year legal warranty from the date of delivery. If your product is defective or does not conform to the contract, you are entitled to:

  • First 12 months: Repair or replacement at no cost to you; defect is presumed to have existed at delivery
  • Months 13–24: Repair or replacement; you must demonstrate the defect existed at delivery
  • If repair or replacement is impossible, disproportionate, or fails: price reduction or full refund

2.2 Australian Statutory Guarantees

Under the Australian Consumer Law (Competition and Consumer Act 2010), products must be of acceptable quality, fit for purpose, and match their description. Where goods fail these guarantees, consumers are entitled to repair, replacement, or refund. Major failures entitle you to choose between a refund and replacement.

2.3 US Consumer Rights

US consumers have rights under applicable state and federal law, including implied warranties of merchantability and fitness for a particular purpose. Nothing in this policy disclaims or limits these implied warranties beyond what is permitted by applicable law.

3. Dornori Standard Return Policy

In addition to your statutory rights, we accept returns of hardware products within 30 days of delivery for any reason, provided the product is in its original, undamaged condition with all accessories and packaging.

4. How to Return

  1. Email support@dornori.com with your order number, reason for return, and whether you prefer a refund or replacement.
  2. We will respond within 5 business days with return instructions and, where applicable, a prepaid return label.
  3. Pack the item securely. Include your order number inside the package.
  4. Send via a tracked shipping method. Retain proof of postage.

5. Refund Processing

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Processing times:

  • Credit/debit cards: 5–10 business days after we receive and inspect the return
  • PayPal: 3–5 business days
  • Other payment methods: As per the payment provider's schedule

We will email you when your refund has been processed. Bank processing times may cause additional delays outside our control.

6. Exchanges

To exchange a product for a different variant or item, please return the original item as described above and place a new order. We do not process direct exchanges due to inventory management constraints.

7. Gifts

Products purchased as gifts may be returned by the gift recipient within the applicable return window. The refund will be issued to the original purchaser's payment method. Gift receipts are included in all orders shipped to an address different from the billing address.

8. Products Sold to or for Children

Products purchased as gifts for children carry the full statutory warranty applicable to hardware goods. Parents and guardians who purchase products for use by children retain all applicable statutory rights.

9. Contact

For all returns, warranty claims, and refund inquiries: support@dornori.com

For EU dispute resolution: ec.europa.eu/consumers/odr
