Returns, Refunds & Warranty

Effective Date: March 31, 2026

Your statutory consumer rights under EU, UK, Australian, and other applicable laws are not limited by this policy. Where any provision herein conflicts with mandatory consumer law, your statutory rights prevail. To initiate a return: support@dornori.com

1. EU & UK — 14-Day Right of Withdrawal

If you are a consumer in the European Union or United Kingdom, you have the right to withdraw from your purchase within 14 calendar days of the date you physically receive the goods, without providing any reason, under the Consumer Rights Directive (2011/83/EU) and UK Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013.

To exercise this right: email support@dornori.com before the 14-day period expires (subject: "Withdrawal Notice — Order #[number]"). Return the goods within 14 days of sending your withdrawal notice. You bear the direct cost of return shipping unless the goods are defective or we shipped incorrectly. We will refund all payments including standard delivery charges within 14 days of receiving the returned goods or proof of return postage, via the original payment method. We may reduce the refund if goods have been handled beyond what is necessary to assess their nature and functioning.

Exceptions: goods made to your personal specification; sealed goods unsuitable for return for hygiene or health reasons where the seal has been broken after delivery; goods inseparably mixed with other items after delivery.

2. Defective Goods & Legal Warranty

EU / UK (2-year statutory warranty): Under EU Directive 2019/771 and the UK Consumer Rights Act 2015, all hardware products carry a minimum 2-year legal warranty from delivery. If your product is defective or does not conform to the contract you are entitled to: repair or replacement at no cost (first 12 months: defect presumed to exist at delivery; months 13–24: you must demonstrate pre-existing defect). If repair or replacement is impossible, disproportionate, or fails: price reduction or full refund.

Australia: Under the Australian Consumer Law (Competition and Consumer Act 2010), products must be of acceptable quality, fit for purpose, and match their description. Major failures entitle you to choose between a refund and replacement.

United States: Implied warranties of merchantability and fitness for a particular purpose apply under applicable state and federal law. Nothing in this policy disclaims these implied warranties beyond what is permitted by law.

3. Dornori Standard Return Policy

In addition to your statutory rights, we accept returns within 30 days of delivery for any reason, provided the product is in its original undamaged condition with all accessories and packaging.

  • EU/UK withdrawal (14 days): Customer pays return shipping (unless defective). Full refund including standard delivery.
  • Dornori goodwill policy (30 days): Customer pays return shipping. Full product price refunded.
  • Defective / DOA product (2-year warranty): Dornori pays return shipping. Repair, replacement, or full refund.
  • Wrong item shipped (30 days): Dornori pays return shipping. Full refund including shipping.

4. How to Return

  1. Email support@dornori.com with your order number, reason for return, and whether you prefer a refund or replacement.
  2. We will respond within 5 business days with return instructions and, where applicable, a prepaid return label.
  3. Pack the item securely and include your order number inside the package.
  4. Ship via a tracked method and retain proof of postage.

5. Refund Processing

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days (cards) or 3–5 business days (PayPal) after we receive and inspect the return. We will email you when your refund is processed. Additional bank processing times may apply.

6. Gifts & Children's Products

Gift recipients may return products within the applicable return window; refunds are issued to the original purchaser's payment method. All products carry the full statutory warranty regardless of whether purchased as a gift for a child.
