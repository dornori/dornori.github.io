Security Policy

Effective Date: March 31, 2026

Found a vulnerability? Report it responsibly to security@dornori.com. We commit to acknowledging your report within 72 hours.

1. Our Security Commitment

Dornori is committed to protecting the security of our website, services, and hardware products, and the Personal Data of our customers — with particular care for data relating to children. We implement industry-standard technical and organisational security measures and continuously review our security posture.

2. Technical Security Measures

Encryption & transport: All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher. HTTP Strict Transport Security (HSTS) is enforced. Sensitive data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 or equivalent. Payment card data is handled exclusively by PCI-DSS Level 1 certified processors.

Infrastructure: Web Application Firewall (WAF) and DDoS mitigation via Cloudflare. Regular vulnerability scanning and patch management. Access to production systems restricted by role-based access controls and multi-factor authentication. Separate development, staging, and production environments.

Application security: Input validation and output encoding throughout. CSRF protection on all state-changing requests. Content Security Policy (CSP) headers implemented. Automated dependency vulnerability scanning.

Hardware product security: Dornori hardware products that connect to digital services are designed with secure boot processes, encrypted communication channels, and over-the-air (OTA) firmware update mechanisms where applicable. Product-specific details are in the product documentation.

3. Organisational Measures

  • Access to Personal Data limited to personnel who require it (need-to-know principle)
  • All staff with data access receive security and privacy awareness training
  • Third-party vendors are vetted for security and bound by Data Processing Agreements
  • An incident response plan is maintained and tested regularly
  • Security reviews conducted before launching new features that process Personal Data

4. Data Breach Response

In the event of a personal data breach we will: contain the breach as quickly as possible; notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware (GDPR / UK GDPR); notify affected individuals without undue delay where the breach is likely to result in high risk to their rights and freedoms; and document all breaches in our internal breach register.

5. Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

We encourage responsible disclosure. If you discover a security issue affecting dornori.com or any Dornori product, please report it by emailing security@dornori.com. Include: a description of the vulnerability and its potential impact; steps to reproduce; any supporting material; and your contact details. You may request our PGP public key for encrypted disclosure.

Our commitments to researchers: We will acknowledge receipt within 72 hours; provide a status update within 14 days; work to resolve valid vulnerabilities within 90 days; notify you when resolved; and not pursue legal action against researchers acting in good faith under these guidelines.

In scope: dornori.com and subdomains, Dornori hardware product firmware and associated APIs, Dornori mobile application (if applicable).
Out of scope: Third-party services (report directly to those vendors), physical attacks, social engineering against staff, denial-of-service attacks.

6. security.txt

7. No Security Guarantees

While we implement reasonable and industry-standard security measures, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee that our Website or services will be entirely free from security vulnerabilities. We will respond promptly to any known issues.
