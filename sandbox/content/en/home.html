The lamp that rises.

"In darkness let's shine a light, to brighten the world and illuminate minds."

The Star‑A is a kinetic, battery-powered outdoor lamp unlike anything else. A pole extends from a compact base — carrying a glowing UFO or Mushroom lamp head up to 190 cm — driven by a real DC motor, controlled by a simple lever switch.

No fire. No power outlet. No app. Just flip a lever and watch it rise.

  • ✅ Genuinely portable — 100 × 100 × 70 mm base
  • ✅ Runs on 3× 18650 batteries
  • ✅ UFO or Mushroom — four colourways each
  • ✅ Build it yourself or buy it ready to go
  • ✅ Open source — free STL files for every part
