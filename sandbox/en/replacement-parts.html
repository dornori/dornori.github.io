⚡ Electrical Components — Professional Grade, Pre-Wired

  • DC Motor — 12V, pre-tested, Faston terminals pre-installed. The heart of the rising mechanism. Replace after heavy use, or upgrade to higher torque to experiment with pole speed.
  • 3-Position Lever Switch — Up–Centre–Down, pre-installed Faston connectors. This is the part that makes children understand switches at a physical, intuitive level. Push up: motor spins one way. Push down: the other. Middle: nothing. That's it. That's a circuit.
  • Main Power Switch with LED — Pre-soldered wire leads, bright LED indicator. Teaches visual feedback: the lamp is on because the light tells you it's on.
  • Strong LED Lamp Head — Pre-wired, mount-ready, warm white output. The thing people look up at. Replace or swap the color temperature with the LED Upgrade Pack.
  • 18650 Battery Holder — Holds 3× protected 18650 cells, Wago-ready leads. The Star-A runs off these — portable, rechargeable, no mains power. Have a spare loaded for longer sessions.
  • Wire Harness Kit — Pre-cut, Faston and ring terminals crimped, color-coded, heat-shrink finished. No soldering needed. No crimping tools. But real, professional-grade wiring your child connects themselves.
  • Wago Connector Set — 6× Wago 221-series lever connectors. Tool-free, reusable, safe from age 6. The same connectors used by electricians worldwide — not a toy version.
  • Hardware Pack — All M3 screws, nuts, and washers in labeled bags, including Nylock nuts for vibration resistance (because the motor vibrates, and that's a real engineering consideration).
